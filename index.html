OFFERS AND INSTRUCTIONS

RULES

At least 50% of the cost must be paid before work starts; turnaround time can be up to three weeks after initial payment.

Payment must be made over PayPal.

Any setting (real life, canon, original, etc.) is fair game!

Pokémon will be drawn on-model; recoloured designs are OK.

Given space constraints, a maximum Pokémon limit (usually 3) will be set depending on the complexity of Pokémon designs.

Original species, humans and animals may be available upon request; references must be provided.

Accessories will be allowed, but extensive clothing will not.

Feel free to provide references for setting, but please understand that they will not be copied as-is unless I am provided a source and explicit permission to copy as I cannot verify usage rights.

Shipping will be through Canada Post and your local public mail service. A quote will be provided if you choose to order the physical copy.

TERMS OF SERVICE

The artist reserves the right to use for all material commissioned, and the client has the right to personal use with appropriate credit in public spaces, as defined by the following conditions for appropriateness: either the name of the artist or a link to the artist's social media must be clearly displayed within the immediate setting of the artwork.

The client may under no circumstances use the commissioned material in commercial settings, including, but not limited to, advertisement, monetised online content, and merchandise.
